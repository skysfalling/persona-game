Inspired by Ingmar Bergman's iconic 1966 film, Persona is a deeply emotional and psychologically rich puzzle game that is a modern exploration of love, guilt, and the fragile human psyche, compellingly crafted to leave you questioning the fine line between reality and perception.

Step into the shoes of Violet, a deeply haunted character caught in a maze of guilt, unrequited love, and the desperate need to confront her tragic past. As she struggles with her perception of reality, her quiet but intensifying interactions with Blue take the players on a surreal yet profoundly human journey.
